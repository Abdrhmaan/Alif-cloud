About Us

Alif Cloud is an Islamic school management software designed and developed to help schools to organize
their information and also to run their school in one place.

Who We Are

Alif Cloud, formerly known as Dugsi Cloud was founded by Duraan Ali in 2016
after spending years in Islamic schools and seeing how unorganized student information was, he started developing Alif Cloud and officially launched it
January 2017.

Our Mission

Allowing every Islamic school to use technology to thrive.
Modernizing old systems of education to catch up with the new world.
Giving school managers, stuff and principals an advantage over other schools
